BIKES & MUSIC CASE STUDY

A study in redesigning a website, desktop and mobile, for a non-profit specializing in providing kids in the Chicago area with bicycle and musical instrument instruction.

RESEARCH

User Interviews

We started by interviewing our users about their philanthropic activities and why they give back to their communities.

User Testing

We asked those users to then use the current website and give us their feedback on its current state, and what they wish it was like.

Data Sythesis

We took the insights from interviews and testing to figure out what changes to prioritize, using an affinity map and prioritization matrix.
